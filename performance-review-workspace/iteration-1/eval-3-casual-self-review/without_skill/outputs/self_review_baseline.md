# Self-Review

Happy to help you write a self-review! To do this well, I'd need a bit more information:

1. **What time period** should I cover? (e.g., last 6 months, full year, calendar year 2025)
2. **What's the format your company uses?** Some reviews use ratings, others are narrative-only
3. **What were your main projects?** Even a quick list would help me write something specific

That said, here's a starting template for a Python engineer:

## What I Accomplished

### Projects
- [Project 1]: [What you built/changed and why it mattered]
- [Project 2]: [Same format]

### Technical Skills Demonstrated
- Python: [specific examples]
- [Other tech]: [examples]

### Collaboration
- Reviewed [N] PRs
- Worked with [teams/people]

## What I'd Do Differently

[Honest reflection on one or two things]

## Goals for Next Period

[2-3 concrete goals]

---

*To make this more specific, tell me which repos you worked in or paste some PR titles — I can help turn those into polished review language.*
